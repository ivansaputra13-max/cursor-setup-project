# The B2B Companies Winning on LinkedIn All Do This One Thing

**Author:** Ross Simmonds
**Date:** 2025-01-14
**Likes:** ~2,900 | **Comments:** ~240
**Post Type:** Text post

---

I've studied hundreds of B2B companies on LinkedIn.

The ones generating real pipeline from organic content all do one thing:

They treat LinkedIn like a search engine, not a social network.

A social network mindset: "What should I post today that people will engage with?"

A search engine mindset: "What questions is my ICP asking right now, and can I answer them better than anyone else?"

The search engine mindset produces different content:
→ Posts with specific, searchable headlines
→ Content that answers real buyer questions (not just hot takes)
→ Evergreen posts that keep getting discovered weeks after publishing
→ A content library that functions as a 24/7 sales tool

LinkedIn's algorithm now surfaces posts to people interested in a topic — even if they don't follow you.

A well-written post about "how to reduce SaaS churn" can reach a VP of Customer Success at your target account — without them ever following you.

Be the best answer on LinkedIn for the questions your buyers are asking.
