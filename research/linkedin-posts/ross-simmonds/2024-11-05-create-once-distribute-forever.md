# Create Once, Distribute Forever — The B2B Content Rule Nobody Follows

**Author:** Ross Simmonds
**Date:** 2024-11-05
**Likes:** ~3,100 | **Comments:** ~260
**Post Type:** Text post

---

Most B2B content teams spend 80% of their time creating and 20% distributing.

It should be the opposite.

You spend 4 hours writing a LinkedIn post.
You publish it.
It reaches 2,000 people over 48 hours.
Then it dies.

What if you distributed it everywhere instead?

That same post becomes:
→ A Twitter/X thread (15 min)
→ A short YouTube video (1 hr)
→ A section of your newsletter (10 min)
→ A Quora answer to a relevant question (15 min)
→ A Reddit comment where someone asked the same thing (10 min)
→ An email to your list (15 min)

Same idea. 6 additional distribution points. 2 hours of extra work.

The best B2B content teams don't produce more content.
They distribute existing content further.

Create once. Distribute forever.
