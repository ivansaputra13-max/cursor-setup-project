# How I Write 5 LinkedIn Posts in 1 Hour

**Author:** Lara Acosta
**Date:** 2025-02-10
**Likes:** ~3,600 | **Comments:** ~290
**Post Type:** Text post

---

Most people spend 2 hours writing one LinkedIn post.
I write 5 in 60 minutes. Here's the system:

**Step 1 — Brain dump (10 min)**
Open a doc. Write every idea, lesson, observation from the past week. No filtering.

**Step 2 — Pick 5 strongest angles (5 min)**
Circle anything that made you think "I wish someone had told me this."

**Step 3 — Write the hook first (10 min)**
For each idea, write 3 possible opening lines. Pick the most tension-creating one.

**Step 4 — Fill the body (25 min)**
5 min per post. Short sentences. One idea per line. White space. Don't over-explain.

**Step 5 — Write the CTA last (10 min)**
End with a question that invites reply, or a one-line insight people want to share.

The secret: writing fast forces you to write simply.
Simple posts outperform complex posts on LinkedIn every time.
