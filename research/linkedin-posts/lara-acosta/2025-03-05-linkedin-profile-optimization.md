# Why Your LinkedIn Profile Is Losing You Followers

**Author:** Lara Acosta
**Date:** 2025-03-05
**Likes:** ~3,900 | **Comments:** ~310
**Post Type:** Text post

---

You can write the best LinkedIn post in the world.
If your profile doesn't convert, nobody follows you.

**The headline.**
"CEO at Company | Helping businesses grow" tells me nothing.
Strong formula: [What you do] + [Who you help] + [Specific outcome]
Example: "I help B2B SaaS founders grow on LinkedIn → 0 to 50K in 18 months"

**The banner.**
Most people leave it blank. Your banner is a billboard.
Use it to state your value prop in one line.

**The About section.**
Don't write a resume. Write a pitch.
First line = hook. Middle = who you help and how. End = one clear CTA.

**The featured section.**
Pin your best post, newsletter signup, or top piece of content.

The test: go to your profile right now.
"If a stranger landed here, would they immediately know why to follow me?"

If no — fix the profile before writing another post.
Your profile is your landing page. Optimize it like one.
