# The 3 Types of LinkedIn Posts That Always Perform

**Author:** Lara Acosta
**Date:** 2024-11-25
**Likes:** ~4,200 | **Comments:** ~340
**Post Type:** Text post

---

After 2 years and 500+ posts, only 3 formats consistently outperform everything else:

**1. The Mistake Post**
"I made this mistake so you don't have to."
Why it works: relatable, honest, positions you as someone who's been in the trenches.
Hook example: "I wasted 6 months posting on LinkedIn before I realized I was doing it completely wrong."

**2. The Framework Post**
"Here's the exact system I use for X."
Why it works: people on LinkedIn are in problem-solving mode. Give them a tool for today.
Hook example: "I grew from 0 to 50K followers using this 3-step system. Here it is."

**3. The Contrarian Post**
"Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong."
Why it works: disagreement creates engagement.
Hook example: "Posting every day will not grow your LinkedIn. Here's what actually does."

Pick one format per week minimum.
Rotate all three for best results.
