# How I Grew to 500K Followers Without Going Viral

**Author:** Justin Welsh
**Date:** 2024-09-03
**Likes:** ~5,800 | **Comments:** ~410
**Post Type:** Text post

---

I've never had a post go viral.

500,000 followers came from doing boring things consistently.

**Post:** 1x per day, Monday–Friday. Every week. No exceptions.

**Engage:** Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting.

**Comment first:** 20 minutes leaving 10 genuine comments on others' content before posting.

**One topic:** I write about one thing — building a one-person business. Algorithms and audiences reward specificity.

**Hook obsession:** I rewrite the first two lines of every post 3–5 times. That's the only line 100% of people read.

The strategy isn't complicated.
The execution is just really, really consistent.

Most people quit in month 2.
That's why month 12 is so valuable.
