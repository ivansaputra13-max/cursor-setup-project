# The Content Matrix — Framework for Endless Post Ideas

**Author:** Justin Welsh
**Date:** 2024-11-12
**Likes:** ~4,100 | **Comments:** ~320
**Post Type:** Text post

---

I used to stare at a blank screen every morning.

No idea what to post. No system. Just hoping inspiration would show up.

Then I built a simple matrix that ended writer's block forever.

Here's the 2x2 Content Matrix I use to generate 20 post ideas in 20 minutes:

**Two content types:**
→ Actionable (teaches something)
→ Inspirational (shifts a belief)

**Two formats:**
→ Personal (from your own experience)
→ Observed (from what you've seen/read)

That gives you 4 quadrants:
1. Actionable + Personal = "Here's how I did X"
2. Actionable + Observed = "Here's how [person/company] did X"
3. Inspirational + Personal = "Here's what I learned from failing at X"
4. Inspirational + Observed = "Here's what [person/company] taught me about X"

Fill each quadrant with 5 ideas from your area of expertise.
That's 20 post ideas. Done.

Stop waiting for inspiration.
Build a system instead.
