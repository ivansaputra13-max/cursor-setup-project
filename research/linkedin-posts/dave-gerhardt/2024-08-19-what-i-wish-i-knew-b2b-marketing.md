# What I Wish I Knew About B2B Marketing When I Started

**Author:** Dave Gerhardt
**Date:** 2024-08-19
**Likes:** ~4,100 | **Comments:** ~310
**Post Type:** Text post

---

10 years in B2B marketing. Here's what actually matters:

**1. Brand is not fluff.**
Companies with the strongest brands have the shortest sales cycles.

**2. Your best marketing asset is your customers.**
One customer story told well outperforms 10 blog posts.

**3. LinkedIn is underpriced.**
Every B2B buyer is on LinkedIn. Most B2B companies are posting garbage there. That gap is your opportunity.

**4. Consistency beats creativity.**
Mediocre content posted consistently outperforms brilliant content posted randomly.

**5. The podcast is the best B2B channel nobody uses seriously.**
Builds deep trust, creates hours of repurposable content, gets you access to anyone you want to interview.

**6. Email still wins.**
Your LinkedIn followers don't belong to you. Your email list does. Always convert LinkedIn audience to email.
