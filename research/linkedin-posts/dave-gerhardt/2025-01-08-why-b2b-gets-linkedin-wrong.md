# Why B2B Companies Keep Getting LinkedIn Wrong

**Author:** Dave Gerhardt
**Date:** 2025-01-08
**Likes:** ~3,400 | **Comments:** ~280
**Post Type:** Text post

---

Most B2B companies treat LinkedIn like a press release channel.

They post:
→ Product updates
→ Award announcements
→ "We're hiring" posts
→ Blog post links with zero context

And then wonder why nobody engages.

**People follow people, not logos.**

Your company page will never out-perform a well-run personal profile. The algorithm knows this. Your buyers know this.

The B2B companies winning on LinkedIn have done one thing: given their founders and executives permission to have a real point of view in public.

That means:
→ Sharing opinions, not just information
→ Talking about failures and hard decisions, not just wins
→ Writing like a human, not a brand guidelines document

LinkedIn is the only platform where B2B buyers are in a professional mindset and open to business content. Don't waste that attention on corporate speak.
