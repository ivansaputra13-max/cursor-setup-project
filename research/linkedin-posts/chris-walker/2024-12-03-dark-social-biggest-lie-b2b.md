# The Biggest Lie in B2B Marketing

**Author:** Chris Walker
**Date:** 2024-12-03
**Likes:** ~3,200 | **Comments:** ~290
**Post Type:** Text post

---

"We can't prove LinkedIn is driving pipeline."

This is the lie B2B marketing teams tell themselves to justify not investing in it.

Here's the truth:

Most B2B buying decisions happen in places you can't track.

A CFO sees your CEO's LinkedIn post during morning scroll.
He mentions it to the VP of Operations in a Slack message.
The VP books a demo 3 weeks later.

Your CRM says: Source = Direct.
You conclude: LinkedIn doesn't work.

This is dark social. And it is responsible for more B2B pipeline than most companies realize.

The fix isn't better tracking.
The fix is asking your customers: "Where did you first hear about us?"

When we ran this question across 200+ B2B SaaS sales calls, LinkedIn was the #1 answer — even when CRM showed "direct."

Stop measuring LinkedIn by last-touch attribution.
Start measuring it by asking people.
