# The LinkedIn Strategy Generating 10M+ Pipeline for B2B SaaS

I've worked with 50+ B2B SaaS companies on GTM strategy.

The ones generating the most pipeline from LinkedIn are doing something most companies refuse to do:

They're giving away their best thinking for free.

Not behind forms. Not gated ebooks. Their actual proprietary frameworks. Posted directly in the feed.

Here's why this works:

When a buyer reads your genuinely useful, specific content on LinkedIn — they don't immediately book a demo.

They save it. They share it internally. They follow you. They come back.

3 weeks later, when budget is approved — they book the demo already convinced.
They've been reading you for weeks.
You don't have to sell them on the problem or your credibility.
You just confirm your product delivers the outcome they already want.

That's a fundamentally different sales conversation.
And it comes from giving your best thinking away on LinkedIn.
