# Demand Generation vs Lead Generation — The Distinction That Changes Everything

**Author:** Chris Walker
**Date:** 2024-09-17
**Likes:** ~4,800 | **Comments:** ~430
**Post Type:** Text post

---

Most B2B SaaS companies are doing lead generation and calling it demand generation.

These are not the same thing.

**Lead generation:** Gate your best content. Capture contact info. Nurture via email. Measure MQLs.

**Demand generation:** Give everything away for free. Make your category so well understood that when buyers are ready, they come already convinced.

LinkedIn organic content is a demand generation channel.

When you post educational content weekly, you are not capturing leads.
You are building a population of buyers who understand their problem better because of you.

These buyers don't fill out forms.
They come inbound when they're ready.
They close faster because the education already happened.

The mistake: measuring LinkedIn by leads generated.
The right metric: inbound demo quality and close rate over time.

Change the metric. Change the strategy.
