# We Almost Went Bankrupt. Here's What Saved Us.

**Author:** Guillaume Moubeche
**Date:** 2024-06-05
**Likes:** ~6,100 | **Comments:** ~520
**Post Type:** Text post

---

In 2019, lemlist had 3 months of runway left.

I did something most founders are too scared to do:
I posted about it publicly on LinkedIn.

Not a "we're struggling but staying positive" post.
A real breakdown: our MRR, our churn, what wasn't working.

That post reached 200,000 people.
We got 400 trial signups in 48 hours.
We didn't go bankrupt.

What I learned:

Vulnerability is not weakness in B2B content. It's differentiation.

Every other SaaS company was posting about their wins. We posted about our near-death experience.

Buyers trusted us more because of it — not less.

The lesson: be honest about the real journey. Buyers can tell the difference between authentic content and marketing polish. They respond to authentic every time.
