# The Exact LinkedIn Post Format That Got lemlist to 1M ARR

**Date:** 2024-04-08
**Likes:** ~5,300 | **Comments:** ~440

When lemlist was early stage, I tested every LinkedIn format possible.

Only one consistently drove signups. I call it the Before/After/Bridge format.

**Before:** Describe the painful situation your buyer is in right now. Be specific.

**After:** Paint the picture of life after the problem is solved.

**Bridge:** Show them how to get from Before to After.

Real example I used:

*Before:* "You're sending 200 cold emails a day. Open rates are decent. Replies? Almost zero."

*After:* "Imagine getting 3-5 qualified replies per day without increasing volume."

*Bridge:* "The fix isn't your subject line. It's your first sentence. Here's what we changed at lemlist that moved reply rate from 3% to 17%..."

This works because it follows the psychology of a buying decision:
First, feel understood → Then, want the outcome → Then, believe the path exists.
