# From 0 to 10M ARR — What LinkedIn Actually Did for lemlist

**Author:** Guillaume Moubeche
**Date:** 2024-08-20
**Likes:** ~4,700 | **Comments:** ~360
**Post Type:** Text post

---

When we started lemlist, we had no money for ads.
No PR budget. No sales team. No connections.

Here's what posting on LinkedIn every day for 3 years generated:

→ Our first 1,000 customers — zero paid acquisition
→ Partnership requests from tools we admired (they found us)
→ Press coverage from journalists following our building-in-public posts
→ Best hires applied because they'd been following us for months
→ 0 CAC on enterprise deals where the buyer had read 50+ of my posts before booking a call

The thing nobody tells you about building in public:

By the time someone books a demo, they already trust you. The sales call isn't a pitch — it's a confirmation.

LinkedIn isn't a marketing channel for us. It's our entire GTM strategy.
