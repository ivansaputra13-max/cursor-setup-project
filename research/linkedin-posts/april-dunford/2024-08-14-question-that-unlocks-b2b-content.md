# The Question That Unlocks All Your B2B Content

**Author:** April Dunford
**Date:** 2024-08-14
**Likes:** ~2,700 | **Comments:** ~210
**Post Type:** Text post

---

There is one question that, if answered well, makes all your B2B marketing easier.

The question: "What would your best customers use if you didn't exist?"

Not "who are your competitors."

What would your best customers — the ones who renew, expand, and refer — actually do if you disappeared tomorrow?

Some would use a spreadsheet.
Some would use a different software category entirely.
Some would hire someone to do it manually.

That answer tells you exactly who you are competing against in the real decision your buyer is making.

And here's why it matters for LinkedIn:

The best B2B LinkedIn posts don't sell your product.
They sell the decision to change from the alternative.

If your best customers choose you over spreadsheets — your content should speak to the pain of spreadsheets.

Stop writing content about your features.
Start writing content about the world your buyer lives in before they find you.

That's the content that converts.
