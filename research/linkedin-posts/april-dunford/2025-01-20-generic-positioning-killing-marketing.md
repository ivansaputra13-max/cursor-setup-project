# Why "We Help Companies Do X Better" Is Killing Your Marketing

**Author:** April Dunford
**Date:** 2025-01-20
**Likes:** ~3,800 | **Comments:** ~310
**Post Type:** Text post

---

Read any B2B SaaS homepage.

Almost all say some version of:
"We help [companies] [do X] [better/faster/cheaper]."

This is positioning that says nothing.

Every competitor says the same thing.
"Better" compared to what?
"Faster" than what baseline?

When your positioning is generic, your LinkedIn content becomes generic too:
→ "5 ways to improve your [process]"
→ "Why [category] matters for your business"
→ "The future of [industry]"

These posts get polite engagement from other vendors.
They get ignored by your actual buyers.

Strong positioning is specific:
"We are the only [category] built for [specific company type] that needs [specific outcome] without [specific tradeoff]"

When you can say that clearly, your LinkedIn content writes itself.

Every post is a proof point for one specific claim.
Every post speaks to one specific pain.
Every post reaches one specific buyer.

Be specific. Be positioned. Be findable.
